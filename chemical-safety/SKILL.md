# Chemical Safety

## Workflow

1. Identify the production unit, material, equipment, operation, and work stage.
2. Separate regulatory requirements, site facts, assumptions, and recommended controls.
3. Check risks across people, equipment, materials, method, environment, and management.
4. Convert hazards into closed-loop actions with owner, deadline, measure, and verification method.
5. Escalate uncertainty when the request requires site-specific engineering judgment or legal compliance review.

## Resource Guide

- Read `references/safety-checklist.md` when preparing inspection or hazard identification outputs.
- Use `assets/inspection-record-template.md` as a reusable field template for safety checks.

## Output Style

- Use concise, auditable language.
- Do not invent site measurements, permits, or monitoring data.
- For high-risk operations, explicitly list assumptions and required on-site confirmation items.
