# Chemical Safety Checklist

## General Inspection Dimensions

- Personnel: qualification, training, PPE, fatigue risk, contractor control.
- Equipment: integrity, interlocks, alarms, grounding, corrosion, leakage.
- Materials: compatibility, storage condition, labeling, SDS availability.
- Process: temperature, pressure, flow, level, sampling, abnormal operation.
- Environment: ventilation, drainage, combustible gas, ignition source control.
- Management: permits, handover, emergency plan, change management, records.

## Closed-Loop Rectification Fields

- Hazard description
- Risk consequence
- Temporary control
- Corrective action
- Responsible person
- Deadline
- Verification evidence
- Residual risk

## High-Risk Work Prompts

- Hot work: gas analysis, isolation, fire watch, nearby combustibles.
- Confined space: ventilation, oxygen/toxic gas test, standby rescue.
- Lifting: load, rigging, command signal, exclusion zone.
- Electrical work: lockout/tagout, discharge, test for absence of voltage.
